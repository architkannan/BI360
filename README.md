## ‚ùó The Problem

**AtliQ Hardwares**, a fast-growing PC and electronics manufacturer, sells across **brick-and-mortar** and **e-commerce** channels in 27 global markets.

As revenues scaled, the company struggled with:

- Fragmented reporting across **sales, finance, supply chain, and marketing**  
- Heavy dependence on **Excel-based** static reports  
- Limited visibility into **Latin American market performance**  
- Slow decision-making compared with analytics-driven competitors like **Dell**

To become more data-driven and support faster strategy decisions, AtliQ needed a **centralized, interactive Power BI reporting system** that all business functions could rely on.

---

## üìù Overview

The project aimed to build a **360¬∞ Power BI analytics suite** integrating multi-year sales, finance, forecasting, and operations data.

### **Goal:**  
Enable executives and functional leaders to **track KPIs, identify performance gaps, improve forecasting, and make data-backed decisions** across the retail and e-commerce business.

### **Key Deliverables**

- Five function-specific dashboards:
  1. **Sales Analytics**  
  2. **Finance Overview**  
  3. **Marketing Insights**  
  4. **Supply Chain Performance**  
  5. **Executive Summary**

- **Core Outcomes:**  
  - Unified view of sales, profit, cost, and operational performance  
  - Reduced reliance on fragmented Excel files  
  - Better visibility into **high-risk regions**, promotions, and pricing  
  - Improved **forecast accuracy** and resource allocation

## üî¢ Data Sources

**Primary Source:** SQL database with dimensional and fact tables  
**Supplementary:** Excel files for competitive benchmarks and financial targets

### **Dimensional Tables**

| Table | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| `dim_customer` | 75 customers across 27 markets (Retail + E-commerce) |
| `dim_market` | 27 markets grouped into 7 sub-zones and 4 global regions |
| `dim_product` | Product hierarchy spanning divisions and categories |

### **Fact Tables**

| Table | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| `fact_sales_monthly` | Actual monthly sales data (2018‚Äì2022) |
| `fact_forecast_monthly` | Forecast quantities for accuracy comparisons |
| Cost Tables | Freight, gross price, manufacturing cost |
| Deduction Tables | Pre-invoice and post-invoice adjustments |

### **Supplementary Data**
- Competitor market share  
- Sales & profit targets  
- Operating expenses
